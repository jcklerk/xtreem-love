♥ Swan boat ♥

Do you feel like enjoying a day on the beautiful, Dutch open waters with your beloved? If that is the case, this trip is perfect for you! The boats can reach a speed of up to 16 km/h or 10 mph. Tickets are purchased at the Swan boat dock prior to boarding the boat. If desired, you can book in advance. The boat can be used from 7am to 7pm.
