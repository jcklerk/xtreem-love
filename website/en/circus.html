♥ Circus ♥

Visit our circus and learn the ways of the ribbon dancers! During this course you will be taught by professional ribbon dancers. The shows are performed throughout the day and you can visit all of them for free! If interested, you can take a look backstage. At the end of the day you may (voluntarily) perform in the show yourself. Is a normal hotel room too boring for you? We also offer an overnight stay in a circus tent!
